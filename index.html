What is the QUICKI calculator?
The QUICKI calculator is a tool that calculates a patient's insulin sensitivity based on their fasting insulin and glucose levels.
How to calculate QUICKI?
To calculate QUICKI you should use the following formula:
QUICKI = 1 / [log(FI) + log(FG)]
Why use QUICKI calculator?
The QUICKI calculator is used in order to determine a patient's insulin sensitivity based on their fasting insulin and glucose levels.
